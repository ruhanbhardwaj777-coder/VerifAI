About VerifAI

How verdicts work and what each one means.

✅

True

Well supported by scientific consensus, peer-reviewed research, or verified historical records.

❌

False

Directly contradicted by established evidence. May be a myth, misconception, or misinformation.

⚠️

Misleading

A grain of truth exists, but important context is missing or distorted. Often the most dangerous.

🔍

Insufficient Evidence

Not enough reliable data to confirm or deny. Doesn't mean false — the evidence is inconclusive.

How it works

1

Submit in any language

Type in Hindi, Urdu, English, Spanish — any language. VerifAI detects it automatically and responds in the same language.

2

Knowledge base first

Checks a curated database of verified claims using exact and fuzzy matching across science, history, and health.

3

AI analysis with fallback

Unknown claims go to an AI model via OpenRouter. 4 models with auto-retry — so one failure doesn't stop you.

4

Relevant links included

Every result includes 3 specific search links — directly tied to the claim — so you can dig deeper yourself.
